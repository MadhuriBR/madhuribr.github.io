Using advanced object recognition technology, AI agent scans the pantry, refrigerator, and garden to identify ingredients, track supplies in real-time, generate alerts for low stock items, and assist in meal planning and grocery shopping.

For accurate scanning and effective object detection, it's helpful to design storage cabinetry with minimal depth, keeping it comfortably within the camera's view.

The customized kitchen design optimizes workflow for both the user and AI, reducing unnecessary movement and enabling precise inventory tracking. This approach enhances AI legibility by clearly displaying items, allowing accurate scanning and tracking, and improving overall user experience within the app.
